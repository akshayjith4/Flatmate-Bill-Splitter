Title = flatemates bill
Description: An app that takes as inputs the amount of a bill for a particular period and the days each of the flatmates stayed in the house during that period. It returns how much each flatmate has to pay. Additionally, it generates a PDF format report stating the names of the flatmates, the period, and how much each of them had to pay.
objects :
       bill:
            amount
            period
       flatmate
            name
            days_in_house
            pays(bill)
       pdf
            filename
            generate(flatmate1,flatmate2,bill)
